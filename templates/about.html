Our Legacy

Three generations of craftsmanship, one unwavering commitment to quality

Building on Generations of Trust

Dosdall Construction LLC represents the culmination of a tradition passed down through generations of skilled craftsmen. Our story begins with my grandfather, who first laid the foundation for what would become Central Minnesota's most trusted construction company.

As a local contractor from a long line of contractors, I understand the unique challenges and opportunities that come with building in Central Minnesota's beautiful landscape. From the harsh winters that test every structure to the stunning lake properties that demand both durability and beauty, we approach each project with the understanding that we're not just building structures—we're creating homes and legacies.

Our commitment goes beyond construction—it's about improving the communities that have shaped us. We take pride in our Central MN heritage and the trust our neighbors place in us to bring their dreams to life, one project at a time.

With over 20 years of experience, we've refined our approach to combine time-tested building methods with cutting-edge techniques, ensuring every structure we build stands as a testament to quality that will endure for generations.
