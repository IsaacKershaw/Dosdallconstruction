Our Story

In Central Minnesota, the Dosdall name has been synonymous with exceptional craftsmanship for generations. Our commitment is rooted in a deep, inherited pride for the structures we build—a legacy passed down through a long line of local builders. We understand that a home is more than just square footage; it is the foundation for your family's future, and we approach every project with this profound responsibility in mind.

We blend this old-world dedication with the best of modern design and construction efficiency. Specializing in luxury lake home additions and complex custom remodels, we leverage cutting-edge materials and precision techniques to deliver spaces that are beautiful, sustainable, and engineered to thrive in the Minnesota climate. Our mission is to take your vision—no matter how ambitious—and translate it into an architectural reality.

As a local contractor, our success is tied directly to the vitality of our community. We operate on a foundation of transparent communication and true partnership, working with you from concept to completion. By choosing Dosdall Construction, you are not just hiring a contractor; you are collaborating with a dedicated team focused on enhancing the homes and lives right here in Central MN.
